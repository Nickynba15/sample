THE INITIATIVE

Below is a form that we have generated to give out to people in relation to an initiative that we are starting. Normali will listen to people and also provide legal as well as therapeutic aid to those who are under various psychological turmoils. We will function initially in the form of a testimony collection unit. You can share with us almost anything and your identity shall remain anonymous as long as you don’t mention your name or the place that you are from, in the testimony that you submit. You would attain legal or therapeutic aid only if you insist us to help you with it, by being ready to come out with your identity. Else, the testimony will be completely anonymous.

The story that you decide to share with us shouldn’t necessarily be your story. It can be something that you saw on the streets, something that someone known to you shared with you and anything that you want the world to know. Anything that’s thought-provoking!

We are deeply concerned about cases related to :
1. Any kind of physical or mental abuse.
2. Domestic violence
3. Child abuse/rights
4. The denial or deprivation of any of the fundamental rights recognised by the Indian constitution

We would be posting all the (only the) anonymous testimonies on our Instagram page so that people could read and relate. You would also know that you are not alone.

It is heart-rending to learn about the radical increase in brutality among humanity. The concrete around us should only be made of love and compassion. We, as humans are obliged to provide knowledge to our fellow lodgers who are deprived of the many things that they deserve. We should also help them lay claim to the latter.

This initiative focuses solely on letting people (especially the poor and the deprived) know their basic rights and chances at freedom. Collection of testimonies is our first step, which would help us learn about the prevailing psychological constraints of mankind.

Thank you so much for your priceless time. Remain a hero!
